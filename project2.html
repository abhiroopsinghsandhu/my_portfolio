PwC Switzerland Power BI Job Simulation

Participated in the PwC Switzerland Power BI Simulation through Forage. Developed three professional dashboards addressing HR insights, customer churn, and call centre metrics. Strengthened my analytical, visualization, and business storytelling abilities.

Project Goals

The goal was to analyze real-world HR datasets and simulate a client engagement experience. I created dashboards to visualize key performance indicators (KPIs), diagnose gender imbalance at the executive level, evaluate churn risk, and assess call centre performance. Deliverables included data-driven insights and concise client-facing email communication.

Example DAX logic used to filter data: Gender = "Female"

Example DAX Measure


% of Female Executives = 
DIVIDE(
  CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(EmployeeData), 
            EmployeeData[Gender] = "Female" && 
            EmployeeData[Level] = "Executive"),
  CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(EmployeeData), 
            EmployeeData[Level] = "Executive")
)

Approach & Tools Used

- Imported and cleaned HR-related data in Power BI
- Built three dashboards:

  • HR Dashboard: Identified root causes of gender imbalance at executive levels
  • Churn Dashboard: Highlighted potential churn trends across departments
  • Call Centre Dashboard: Evaluated performance metrics like average handling time and satisfaction rate

Insights & Results

The analysis uncovered gender-related disparities in promotion pipelines, helping frame diversity discussions at leadership levels. Churn patterns revealed specific risk-prone segments, and call centre metrics informed staffing and training recommendations. The project emphasized the importance of clear visuals and concise business communication.
